My Career Options

Data Analytics

In this fast-paced world of digital marketing and cloud engineers, data analysts have merged

improve my understanding throughout my years of work. Moreover, since this career is centred

Back-End Developer

As a person with a keen interest in innovation, programming, and software development, no other job

Mobile App Development

Ever since mobile phones have gained world-wide popularity within the last decades, there has

Software Engineer

Starting with the fact that software engineering is one of the most growing fields in the ICT field, there

quick fix for some cases or more complex strategic solutions for others. Additionally, the teamwork
and leadership skills I have acquired during group projects at SP will definitely come in handy in this
