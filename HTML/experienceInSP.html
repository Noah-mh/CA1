Current Experiences

Adjusting at first seemed to be a mission. Everything was new for an international student like myself to adjust to living in the new city.
However, the moment I participated in SP online camp orientation, I was certain that this was going to be my second home for the next
three years. During the orientation, we were able to partake in a variety of games as an ice-breaker between the students and teachers.
We were also introduced to the wide range of workshops, and projects that were in place at SP. The karate workshop, in particular, stood
out to me. Taking an interest in the club, I participated in the training provided by my senior senseis and gained plenty of new experiences
and pleasant memories.

Courses Offered In SOC

Diploma in Information Technology

Choosing to study the Diploma in Information Technology was a perfect fit for me
as a person fascinated by all the modern technologies that the world has come to.
By joining this program, I have gathered not only a great deal of bookish
knowledge but also handfuls of real-life experience on different sectors concerning
Information Technology. This course provides me with knowledge on a variety of
sectors such as Enterprise Systems Development, Software Applications, and Data
Engineering. Different electives from this Diploma include Software Development,
Immersive Simulation Technology, and User Experience (UX) Design.
